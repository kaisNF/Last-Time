Bio

This paragraph provides information about the company NorthsideA. I picked the name NorthsideA because I am from Tunisia, which is on the north side of Africa, and I am currently studying in Canada, which is on the north side of America. I thought it would be cleverÂ to name the company this way while still having a meaning behind it.

Northside A is a company that enables beginners learn music theory and composition techniques by engaging one of the teachers offered on the website. This allows music producers and sound engineers to broaden their knowledge and horizons in the music field. Users can buy equipment, instruments, and music production kits from Northside A. It also features a community component where novice music producers may share their work and seek help from experts. Last but not least, Northside A would assist its users in finding locations for their events by providing them the space needed for their gig.

The target audience for NorthsideA are people who are passionate and intrested in music production. Also, it is a space where professionals and beginners can be together, as for the professionals they can have a source of income through the website by teaching the beginners, as for the beginners they can start their music career by the courses offered, the gear suggested and the opportunity to share their music in the venues provided by the brand.
